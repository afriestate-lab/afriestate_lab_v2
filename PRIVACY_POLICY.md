# Privacy Policy for Icumbi Mobile App

**Last Updated:** August 18, 2025  
**Effective Date:** August 18, 2025  
**App Version:** 1.0.0

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to Icumbi ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use the Icumbi mobile application ("App").

By using our App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use our App.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Personal Information
We collect the following personal information when you use our App:

- **Full Name**: Your complete name as provided during registration
- **Phone Number**: Your 10-digit Rwandan phone number (primary identifier)
- **Email Address**: Your email address for account verification and communications
- **Password**: Securely encrypted password for account authentication
- **Role**: Your role in the platform (tenant, landlord, manager, admin)
- **Profile Picture**: Optional avatar image (if provided)

### 2.2 Property and Rental Information
When using our property management features, we collect:

- **Property Details**: Property names, addresses, descriptions, and specifications
- **Property Images**: Photos of properties and rooms (with your consent)
- **Rental Information**: Lease terms, payment amounts, and rental history
- **Location Data**: Property addresses and general location information

### 2.3 Payment Information
For payment processing, we collect:

- **Payment Method**: Your chosen payment method (MTN MoMo, Airtel Money, Bank Transfer, Cards, Cash)
- **Payment Amounts**: Transaction amounts and payment history
- **Transaction IDs**: Unique identifiers for payment tracking
- **Phone Numbers**: For mobile money payments (Airtel phone numbers)

### 2.4 Device and Technical Information
We automatically collect:

- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system, and app version
- **Usage Data**: App usage patterns, features accessed, and interaction data
- **Network Information**: Internet connection type and network status
- **Crash Reports**: App performance and error data (anonymized)

### 2.5 Location Information
With your explicit consent, we may collect:

- **GPS Location**: Precise location for property search and mapping
- **Location Permissions**: Access to location services for nearby property features

### 3.2 Property Management

### 3.3 Payment Processing
- Process rental payments and transactions
- Generate payment receipts and records
- Track payment history and financial reports
- Handle payment disputes and refunds

### 3.4 App Functionality
- Provide core app features and services
- Improve app performance and user experience
- Debug technical issues and maintain app stability
- Ensure app security and prevent fraud

### 3.5 Communication
- Send important notifications about your account
- Provide customer support and assistance
- Share updates about app features and services
- Send payment reminders and lease notifications
