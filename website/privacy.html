Privacy Policy

Icumbi Mobile App

Last Updated: August 18, 2025 | Version: 1.0.0

Welcome to Icumbi! This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use the Icumbi mobile application. By using our App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

We collect the following personal information when you use our App:

  • Full Name: Your complete name as provided during registration
  • Phone Number: Your 10-digit Rwandan phone number (primary identifier)
  • Email Address: Your email address for account verification and communications
  • Password: Securely encrypted password for account authentication
  • Role: Your role in the platform (tenant, landlord, manager, admin)
  • Profile Picture: Optional avatar image (if provided)

Property and Rental Information

When using our property management features, we collect:

  • Property Details: Property names, addresses, descriptions, and specifications
  • Property Images: Photos of properties and rooms (with your consent)
  • Rental Information: Lease terms, payment amounts, and rental history
  • Location Data: Property addresses and general location information

Payment Information

For payment processing, we collect:

  • Payment Method: Your chosen payment method (MTN MoMo, Airtel Money, Bank Transfer, Cards, Cash)
  • Payment Amounts: Transaction amounts and payment history
  • Transaction IDs: Unique identifiers for payment tracking
  • Phone Numbers: For mobile money payments (Airtel phone numbers)

2. How We Use Your Information

Account Management

  • Create and manage your user account
  • Authenticate your identity and maintain account security
  • Provide personalized user experience based on your role
  • Send account-related notifications and updates

Property Management

  • Display and manage property listings
  • Process property bookings and reservations
  • Handle tenant-landlord communications
  • Generate property reports and analytics

Payment Processing

  • Process rental payments and transactions
  • Generate payment receipts and records
  • Track payment history and financial reports
  • Handle payment disputes and refunds

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:
  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Authentication: Secure login and multi-factor authentication
  • Access Controls: Limited access to personal information
  • Regular Audits: Security assessments and vulnerability testing

4. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: View and download your personal information
  • Correct: Update or modify your information
  • Delete: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Portability: Export your data in a machine-readable format

5. Information Sharing

We may share your information with:

  • Service Providers: Payment processors, cloud storage, and analytics partners
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect safety
  • With Your Consent: Only when you explicitly agree

6. Third-Party Services

Our payment processing partners include:

  • MTN Mobile Money
  • Airtel Money
  • Banking Partners
  • Card Processors

7. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes through in-app notifications, email, or website updates.
