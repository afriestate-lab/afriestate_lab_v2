# Iteganyabikorwa rya Ubwihisho - Porogaramu ya Icumbi

**Igihe Byahindutse:** Kanama 18, 2025  
**Itangira:** Kanama 18, 2025  
**Verisiyo ya Porogaramu:** 1.0.0

## 1. Intangiriro

Murakaza neza kuri Icumbi ("twebwe," "ibyacu," cyangwa "twe"). Iyi Iteganyabikorwa rya Ubwihisho isobanura uko dukusanya, dukoresha, dushyira kandi dukurenganura amakuru yawe ya gusobanura igiti igihe ukoresha porogaramu ya Icumbi ("Porogaramu").

Ukoresheje porogaramu yacu, uyemera kusanywa no gukoreshwa kw'amakuru mu buryo bw'iyi teganyabikorwa. Niba utemeye iyi teganyabikorwa, ntabwo ukoreshe porogaramu yacu.

## 2. Amakuru Dukusanya

### 2.1 Amakuru ya Gusobanura Igiti
Dukusanya amakuru ya gusobanura igiti aya:

- **Amazina Yuzuye**: Amazina yawe yuzuye nk'uko watanze igihe wiyandikisha
- **Nimero ya Telefoni**: Nimero yawe ya telefoni ya Rwanda y'imibare 10 (ikimenyetso gikomeye)
- **Imeri**: Aderesi yawe ya imeri yo gukemura konti no gutumira ubutumwa
- **Ijambo ry'Ibanga**: Ijambo ry'ibanga rikurikiranwa neza ryo gukemura konti
- **Uruhare**: Uruhare rwawe muri porogaramu (umukodesha, nyir'inyubako, umuyobozi, umugenzuzi mukuru)
- **Ifoto ya Konti**: Ifoto yawe ya avatar (niba watanze)

### 2.2 Amakuru y'Inyubako no Kwishyura
Igihe ukoresha ibikorwa byacu byo gukurikirana inyubako, dukusanya:

- **Ibisobanura by'Inyubako**: Amazina y'inyubako, aderesi, ibisobanura, n'ibisobanura
- **Amashusho y'Inyubako**: Amashusho y'inyubako n'amavumba (niba wemeye)
- **Amakuru yo Kwishyura**: Amabwiriza yo kwishyura, amafaranga yo kwishyura, n'amateka yo kwishyura
- **Amakuru y'Aho**: Aderesi z'inyubako n'amakuru y'aho y'ibanze

### 2.3 Amakuru yo Kwishyura
Kugira ngo dukore ubwishyu, dukusanya:

- **Uburyo bwo Kwishyura**: Uburyo bwo kwishyura wahitamo (MTN MoMo, Airtel Money, Amabanki, Amakarita, Amafaranga)
- **Amafaranga yo Kwishyura**: Amafaranga y'ubwishyu n'amateka yo kwishyura
- **Ibimenyetso by'Ubwishyu**: Ibimenyetso bihambaye byo gukurikirana ubwishyu
- **Nimero za Telefoni**: Kugira ngo dukore ubwishyu bwa mobile money (nimero za Airtel)

### 2.4 Amakuru y'Igikoresho n'Ibikorwa
Dukusanya mu buryo bwa gahunda:

- **Amakuru y'Igikoresho**: Ubwoko bw'igikoresho, porogaramu ikoreshwa, n'uburyo bw'uburyo bw'uburyo
- **Amakuru yo Gukoresha**: Uburyo bwo gukoresha porogaramu, ibikorwa byakoreshejwe, n'amakuru yo gufatanya
- **Amakuru y'Ubutumwa**: Ubwoko bw'ubutumwa bwa interineti n'uburyo bw'ubutumwa
- **Raporo z'Ikosa**: Ibikorwa by'uburyo n'amakuru y'ikosa (bitazwi)

### 2.5 Amakuru y'Aho
Niba wemeye neza, dushobora gusanya:

- **Aho ya GPS**: Aho yuzuye kugira ngo ushake inyubako no gufata amashusho
- **Uburenganzira bwo Gukoresha Aho**: Kwinjira mu bikorwa by'aho kugira ngo dukore ibikorwa by'inyubako ziri hafi

## 3. Uko Dukoresha Amakuru Yawe

### 3.1 Gukurikirana Konti
- Kurema no gukurikirana konti yawe
- Kugenzura ubwishingizi bwawe no gukurikirana ubwishingizi bwa konti
- Gutanga uburyo bwo gukoresha porogaramu bwa gusobanura igiti ku giti ku buryo bwa uruhare rwawe
- Gutumira ubutumwa bw'ibanze n'uburyo bw'ibanze

### 3.2 Gukurikirana Inyubako
- Kwerekana no gukurikirana amashusho y'inyubako
- Gukora ubwishyu bwo kwishyura no kwishyura
- Gukurikirana ubutumwa bwa umukodesha-nyir'inyubako
- Gutanga raporo z'inyubako n'ibisobanura

### 3.3 Gukora Ubwishyu
- Gukora ubwishyu bwo kwishyura n'ubwishyu
- Gutanga amakuru yo kwishyura n'amateka
- Kurondora amateka yo kwishyura n'uburyo bw'uburyo
- Gukurikirana amakimbirane yo kwishyura no gusubiza

### 3.4 Ibikorwa bya Porogaramu
- Gutanga ibikorwa by'ibanze bya porogaramu n'uburyo
- Gutera imbere uburyo bwo gukoresha porogaramu n'uburyo bwo gukoresha
- Gukosora ibibazo by'uburyo no gukurikirana uburyo bwa porogaramu
- Gukurikirana ubwishingizi bwa porogaramu no kurwanya uburiganya

### 3.5 Gutumira Ubutumwa
- Gutumira ubutumwa bw'ibanze kuri konti yawe
- Gutanga ubufasha n'ubufasha
- Gutumira ubutumwa bw'uburyo bw'uburyo n'uburyo
- Gutumira ibibutso byo kwishyura n'ubutumwa bwo kwishyura

## 4. Gusangira no Kwerekana Amakuru

### 4.1 Niba Wemeye
Dushobora gusangira amakuru yawe na ba gatanu gusa igihe wemeye neza ko ubyemeye.

### 4.2 Abatanga Uburyo
Dushobora gusangira amakuru na ba batanga uburyo bwa gushimishije abadufasha mu:

- **Gukora Ubwishyu**: MTN MoMo, Airtel Money, n'aba gatanu b'amabanki
- **Ububiko bwa Cloud**: Supabase kugira ngo dushyire amakuru neza
- **Ibisobanura**: Ibisobanura byo gukoresha porogaramu no gukurikirana uburyo
- **Ubufasha bw'Abakoresha**: Gukurikirana tiketi z'ubufasha n'ubufasha bw'abakoresha

### 4.3 Ibisabwa n'Iteganyabikorwa
Dushobora kwerekana amakuru yawe igihe bisabwa n'amateganyabikorwa, harimo:

- **Ibikorwa by'Iteganyabikorwa**: Amabwiriza ya urukiko, subpoenas, cyangwa ibisabwa by'amateganyabikorwa
- **Inzego za Leta**: Inzego z'ubutegetsi cyangwa ibigo byo gukurikirana
- **Ubwishingizi n'Ubwishingizi**: Kugira ngo dukurenganure ubwishingizi bw'abakoresha cyangwa dukurenganure uburiganya
- **Gukurikirana**: Kugira ngo dukurikirane amateganyabikorwa n'amateganyabikorwa

### 4.4 Guhindura Ibikorwa
Mu gihe cyo guhuza, gufata, cyangwa kugurisha ibikorwa, amakuru yawe ashobora guhindurwa kuri ikigo gishya, kandi bigaragara ko ubwishingizi bwa gusobanura igiti bukurikiranwa.

## 5. Ubwishingizi bw'Amakuru

### 5.1 Uburyo bw'Ubwishingizi
Dukoresha uburyo bw'ubwishingizi bw'uburyo bw'uburyo kugira ngo dukurenganure amakuru yawe:

- **Gufungura**: Amakuru yose afungurwa mu buryo bwo gutumira no gukurikirana
- **Gukemura**: Kwinjira neza no gukemura ibintu bibiri
- **Kwinjira mu Bikorwa**: Kwinjira mu bikorwa by'amakuru ya gusobanura igiti
- **Gukurikirana Rusange**: Ibisuzo by'ubwishingizi no gukurikirana ibibazo

### 5.2 Gukurikirana Amakuru
Dukurikirana amakuru yawe kugeza igihe gikenewe kugira ngo:

- Tugire uburyo bwawe no gukurikirana konti yawe
- Dukurikirane amateganyabikorwa
- Dukosore amakimbirane no gukurikirana amasezerano
- Dutere imbere uburyo bwawe no gukoresha porogaramu

### 5.3 Gusiba Amakuru
Ushobora gusaba gusiba konti yawe n'amakuru yawe ya gusobanura igiti ukoresheje support@icumbi.com. Tuzakora ibisabwa byawe mu minsi 30.

## 6. Uburenganzira Bwawe n'Amahitamo

### 6.1 Kwinjira no Gukurikirana
Ufite uburenganzira bwo:

- **Kwinjira**: Reba no gufata amakuru yawe ya gusobanura igiti
- **Gukosora**: Gutera imbere cyangwa guhindura amakuru yawe
- **Gusiba**: Gusaba gusiba konti yawe n'amakuru
- **Gufata**: Gufata amakuru yawe mu buryo bwa gahunda

### 6.2 Amahitamo yo Gutumira Ubutumwa
Ushobora gukurikirana uko dutumira ubutumwa:

- **Ubutumwa bwa Imeri**: Hitamo kwinjira/gusohoka mu ubutumwa bwa imeri
- **Ubutumwa bwa Porogaramu**: Kurikirana igenamiterere rya ubutumwa bwa porogaramu
- **Ubutumwa bwa SMS**: Kurikirana ubutumwa bwa SMS bwo kwishyura

### 6.3 Ibikorwa by'Aho
Ushobora kurikirana kwinjira mu aho:

- **Gushyira mu Bikorwa/Gusiba**: Guhindura ibikorwa by'aho on/off
- **Gukurikirana Uburenganzira**: Kurikirana uburenganzira bwa aho mu igenamiterere rya gikoresho
- **Kurikirana Rusange**: Hitamo ibikorwa bishobora kwinjira mu aho

## 7. Ubwishingizi bw'Abana

Porogaramu yacu ntiyateguwe kuri abana bari munsi y'imyaka 13. Ntidusanya amakuru ya gusobanura igiti ku bana bari munsi y'imyaka 13. Niba uri umubyeyi cyangwa umwishingizi kandi ufata ko umwana wawe yatuzuza amakuru ya gusobanura igiti, twandikire vuba.

## 8. Gutumira Amakuru mu Mahanga

Amakuru yawe ashobora gutumirwa kandi akoreshwa mu mahanga atari Rwanda. Dukuraho uburyo bw'ubwishingizi bwemewe kugira ngo dukurenganure amakuru yawe igihe dutumira mu mahanga.

## 9. Ibikorwa bya Ba Gatanu

### 9.1 Abakora Ubwishyu
Ba gatanu bacu b'uburyo bwo gukora ubwishyu harimo:

- **MTN Mobile Money**: Ubwishyu bwa mobile money
- **Airtel Money**: Ubwishyu bwa mobile money
- **Ba Gatanu b'Amabanki**: Gutumira amabanki ya gusanzwe
- **Abakora Amakarita**: Ubwishyu bwa kare y'amabanki

### 9.2 Ibisobanura no Kurikirana
Dukoresha ibikorwa bya ba gatanu kugira ngo:

- **Ibisobanura bya Porogaramu**: Uburyo bwo gukoresha no kurikirana uburyo
- **Raporo z'Ikosa**: Kurondora ikosa no uburyo bwa porogaramu
- **Ubufasha bw'Abakoresha**: Help desk no gukurikirana ubufasha

## 10. Guhindura Iyi Teganabikorwa

Dushobora gutera imbere iyi Iteganyabikorwa rya Ubwihisho buri gihe. Tuzamenya buri guhindura gikomeye dukoresheje:

- **Ubutumwa bwa Porogaramu**: Kwerekana ubutumwa muri porogaramu
- **Ubutumwa bwa Imeri**: Gutumira imeri ku aderesi yawe yanditswe
- **Gutera Imbere kuri Website**: Gutera imbere teganyabikorwa kuri website yacu

## 11. Amakuru yo Gutumira Ubutumwa

Niba ufite ibibazo kuri iyi Iteganyabikorwa rya Ubwihisho cyangwa ibikorwa byacu by'amakuru, twandikire:

**Aderesi:** Kigali, Rwanda  
**Website:** https://icumbi.com

## 12. Amakimbirane n'Amakimbirane

Niba ufite ibibazo kuri ibikorwa byacu by'amakuru, ushobora:

1. **Kutwandikira**: Twandikire ubufasha bwacu bw'ubwishingizi
2. **Ikigo rya Gukurikirana Amakuru**: Gutanga ikimbirane ku kigo gikwiye
3. **Ibikorwa by'Iteganyabikorwa**: Gukora ibikorwa by'iteganyabikorwa nk'uko bishoboka

## 13. Amateganyabikorwa

Iyi Iteganyabikorwa rya Ubwihisho ikurikiranwa n'amateganyabikorwa ya Rwanda. Buri makimbirane ava muri iyi teganyabikorwa azakosorwa mu buryo bw'amateganyabikorwa ya Rwanda.

## 14. Kwemera

Ukoresheje porogaramu ya Icumbi, uyemera kusanywa, gukoreshwa, no gusangirwa kw'amakuru yawe nk'uko byasobanuwe muri iyi Iteganyabikorwa rya Ubwihisho.
