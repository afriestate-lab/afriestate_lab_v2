# Privacy Policy Summary - Icumbi Mobile App

## What We Collect
- **Personal Info**: Name, phone number, email, role
- **Property Data**: Property details, photos, rental information
- **Payment Info**: Payment methods, transaction amounts
- **Device Data**: App usage, device information, location (with consent)

## How We Use It
- **Account Management**: Create and manage your account
- **Property Services**: Handle property listings and rentals
- **Payment Processing**: Process rental payments
- **App Improvement**: Enhance app features and performance
- **Communication**: Send important notifications

## Data Sharing
- **With Consent**: Only when you explicitly agree
- **Service Providers**: Payment processors, cloud storage, analytics
- **Legal Requirements**: When required by law
- **Business Transfers**: In case of company changes

## Your Rights
- **Access**: View your personal information
- **Correct**: Update your information
- **Delete**: Request account deletion
- **Control**: Manage communication preferences

## Security
- **Encryption**: All data encrypted in transit and at rest
- **Authentication**: Secure login systems
- **Access Controls**: Limited data access
- **Regular Audits**: Security assessments
